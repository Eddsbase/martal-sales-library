Objection Handling Vault

Reframe, Don't Rebut

Your goal isn't to "win" — it's to understand what's behind the no, and turn that into relevance.

📁 Full Vault Folder 📄 Full Objection Handling Doc

1. PAIN Problem

"We're happy with our current provider." / "Honestly, we're doing fine right now."

They're satisfied — no pain. Your job is to surface latent pain they haven't articulated yet.

2. TIMING Problem

"We're too busy right now." / "We're waiting until next quarter." / "This isn't a priority."

Not now / too busy / later. Reframe around what waiting actually costs them.

3. MONEY Problem

"We don't have budget." / "We're under contract for another year."

No budget / locked-in / cost issues. Shift from cost to cost of inaction.

4. BRUSH-OFF Problem

"Send me something and I'll take a look." / "Can you just email me some info?"

Send info / email me. They're trying to end the call. Earn 30 more seconds of relevance.

5. FEAR OF CHANGE Problem

"We're not looking to switch platforms." / "We've got something built in-house."

Switching is scary. Acknowledge the risk and reframe as augmentation, not replacement.

6. SKEPTICISM Problem

"We've tried something like this before and it didn't work."

Tried before, didn't work. Validate their experience and differentiate what's different this time.

7. DECISION-MAKING Problem

"I need to run this by my team."

Find out if this is a real next step or a polite exit. Help them build the internal case.
