Objection Handling Vault

Reframe

Your goal isn't to "win" — it's to understand what's behind the no and turn it into relevance.

📁 Vault Folder 📄 Full Doc

1. PAIN Problem

"We're happy with our current provider."

They're satisfied. Surface latent pain they haven't articulated yet.

2. TIMING Problem

"We're too busy right now." / "Next quarter."

Reframe around what waiting actually costs them.

3. MONEY Problem

"We don't have budget." / "Under contract."

Shift from cost to cost of inaction.

4. BRUSH-OFF Problem

"Send me something and I'll take a look."

They're trying to end the call. Earn 30 more seconds of relevance.

5. FEAR OF CHANGE

"We're not looking to switch platforms."

Acknowledge risk. Reframe as augmentation, not replacement.

6. SKEPTICISM Problem

"We've tried this before and it didn't work."

Validate their experience. Differentiate what's different now.

7. DECISION-MAKING

"I need to run this by my team."

Find out if it's a real next step or a polite exit. Help build the case.
